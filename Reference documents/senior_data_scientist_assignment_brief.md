# Senior Data Scientist Assignment Brief

## Objective

As a **Senior Data Scientist** in the **Data Analytics and Research Branch**, you will be expected to:

- Extract and integrate data from multiple sources, conduct analysis, and develop reports and predictive models to support decision-making.
- Lead data solution projects, including the design and implementation of end-to-end automated systems for data collection, analysis, and reporting.
- Lead and execute complex analytical initiatives requiring advanced statistical expertise, as well as knowledge of machine learning (ML) and large language model (LLM) applications.

---

## Assignment Context

The Ministry’s current **Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) case management system** is primarily designed for enforcement and compliance across four sectors:

- Construction
- Industry
- Health Care
- Mining

The system captures information reported directly to the Ministry or gathered during inspection visits.

This includes structured and open text data such as:

- Incident or inspection date
- Location
- Sector
- Workplace observations
- Non-compliance findings and issued orders
- Inspection summaries

A full data dictionary is provided for reference.

---

## Supporting Materials

In addition to structured data and open text fields, inspection visits may include supporting materials such as:

- Photos
- Videos
- Inspection reports

These materials may contain:

- Personal information
- Detailed workplace context
- Incident descriptions
- Worker information
- Injury details

Sample inspection reports are provided.

---

## Business Problem

The Ministry aims to become more proactive in identifying **high-risk organizations** and targeting inspections more effectively.

The goal is to optimize the use of limited resources by improving the Ministry’s ability to identify workplaces that may require inspection, intervention, or additional monitoring.

To support this objective, the Ministry is looking for different skillsets. You may choose **one** of the following options.

---

# Option 1: Predictive Risk Identification

## Goal

Using available reports and historical data:

- Identify key risk factors.
- Develop a predictive model or analytical approach to identify high-risk organizations.

## Focus

This option emphasizes:

- Analytical capability
- Statistical modeling
- Application of ML and LLM techniques

## Optional External Data Sources

You may incorporate additional data from publicly available sources to strengthen the analysis, such as:

- Ontario Data Catalogue
- Statistics Canada
- Other jurisdictions

---

# Option 2: End-to-End Data Solution Design

## Goal

Design a new, automated, end-to-end data solution that:

- Captures additional data not currently collected in the case management system.
- Integrates existing and new data sources.
- Supports the development of a predictive model to assess workplace safety risk.

## Focus

This option emphasizes:

- Digital solution design
- Data integration
- Data modeling
- Advanced analytics
