enforcement data into compelling stories and actionable policies. If you have the tenacity to seek answers through data,
we want to hear from you.

About the job
As a Senior Data Scientist, you will:
• Lead in data management, statistical modelling, and system enhancement.
• Serve as the data and digital solution expert on committees and governance bodies.
• Guide project teams, set standards, and provide training.
• Manage large, complex data and digital projects.
• Conduct analysis and develop predictive models and tools for planning.
• Investigate opportunities in improving occupational health and safety and employment standard systems, planning,
and policy-making.
• Prepare briefings and responses to information requests.
• Offer expertise to clients and stakeholders.
What you bring to the team

Technical Knowledge and Data Skills:
You have:
• Knowledge of performance indicators to advise clients and analyze data.
• Understanding of data management, business analysis, database architecture, and data visualization.
• Programming skills in big data, statistical modelling, and visual analytics;
• Knowledge of relevant software and the ability to lead and advise on I&IT issues.
• Knowledge of data privacy standards and laws (e.g., FIPPA, PHIPPA).
Leadership and Project Delivery Skills:
You can:
• Lead and plan multiple projects, collaborate with team members, and manage deadlines.
• Demonstrate strong leadership and team-building skills to guide staff.
• Plan, prioritize, and coordinate activities effectively.
Research and Analytical Skills:
You can:
• Use analytical methods and tools effectively.
• Lead program reviews with strong research and analytical skills.
• Anticipate and address emerging issues.
• Analyze large, complex datasets with various tools.
• Evaluate qualitative and quantitative information to assess changes.
Communication and Stakeholder Management Skills:
You can:
• Provide technical expertise and advice on project planning and reporting.
• Manage stakeholder relations and advise clients on solutions.
• Achieve consensus and influence discussions with stakeholders.
• Communicate effectively in writing and orally to present reports and explain data issues.
• Develop and maintain relationships with clients, stakeholders, and colleagues.
• Prepare reports, make presentations, and provide recommendations to senior management.
